# Pasta Chickpea Pomodoro
<!-- quick:18 -->

![Pasta Chickpea Pomodoro](images/saturday_evening_1.jpg)

Cook {180g {pasta}} until just shy of done. Meanwhile, simmer {200g {tomato}} with {5g {garlic}}, {12g {olive_oil}}, {1g {oregano}}, and {1g {aleppo_pepper}}. Crush about half of {130g {chickpea}} into the tomato so the sauce turns thick and silky; leave the rest whole. Splash in {8g {balsamic_vinegar}}. Toss the pasta in the sauce with a splash of pasta water until glossy. Finish with {8g {basil}} and {8g {parmesan_cheese}}.

# Chicken Quinoa Bowl
<!-- quick:18 -->

![Chicken Quinoa Bowl](images/sunday_midday_2.jpg)

Cook {120g {quinoa}}. Whisk {20g {tahini}} with {8g {lemon}} juice, {5g {olive_oil}}, {2g {cumin}}, and a splash of water until pourable. Pan-sear {130g {chicken}} in {5g {olive_oil}} with {1g {smoked_paprika}} until browned. Toss warm quinoa with {100g {broccoli}} (steamed), {70g {bell_pepper}} (thin strips), and most of the tahini sauce so it coats rather than sits on top. Slice the chicken over, spoon on remaining sauce, and finish with {10g {pumpkin_seed}} and {5g {parsley}}.

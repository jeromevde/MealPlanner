# Natto Kimchi Rice
<!-- quick:10 -->

![Natto Kimchi Rice](images/friday_midday_1.jpg)

Chop {60g {kimchi}} fine. In a bowl, stir {80g {natto}} vigorously with {10g {mayonnaise}}, {5g {mustard}}, {8g {soy_sauce}}, {5g {gochujang}}, and {5g {sesame_oil}} until it turns creamy and sticky (this is what makes natto eatable). Fold in the kimchi. Warm {150g {brown_rice}} with roasted {80g {sweet_potato}} cubes, then fold the creamy natto through the rice so it coats the grains. Finish with {3g {nori}}, {5g {sesame_seed}}, and {8g {scallion}}.

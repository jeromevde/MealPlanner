# Liver Bolognese
<!-- quick:20 -->

![Liver Bolognese](images/monday_evening_1.jpg)

Finely chop {80g {onion}} and {5g {garlic}}. Sauté in {15g {olive_oil}} until soft. Add {150g {liver}} (chopped) and {100g {beef}}, cook 5 minutes. Stir in {200g {tomato}} (crushed) and simmer 10 minutes. Toss with {180g {pasta}} and serve with {10g {parmesan_cheese}}.

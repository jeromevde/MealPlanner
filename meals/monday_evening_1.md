# Chicken Veggie Tray Bake
<!-- quick:25 -->

![Chicken Veggie Tray Bake](images/monday_evening_1.jpg)

Toss {200g {sweet_potato}}, {150g {broccoli}}, and {150g {chicken}} with {15g {olive_oil}}, {3g {garlic}}, {2g {smoked_paprika}}, {2g {thyme}}, zest from {5g {lemon}}, and {1g {salt}} so everything shares the same seasoning. Roast at 200°C for 22 minutes. Stir {60g {yogurt}} with {5g {lemon}} juice, {2g {garlic}}, and a pinch of salt into a cool sauce, then spoon it over the hot tray so it melts into the pan juices.

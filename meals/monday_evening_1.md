# Lentil Tomato Pasta
<!-- quick:18 -->

![Lentil Tomato Pasta](images/monday_evening_1.jpg)

Cook {180g {pasta}}. Sauté {60g {onion}} and {5g {garlic}} in {15g {olive_oil}}. Add {120g {lentil}} (cooked), {200g {tomato}}, and {2g {cumin}}. Simmer 8 minutes and toss with pasta. Top with {10g {parmesan_cheese}} and {5g {parsley}}.

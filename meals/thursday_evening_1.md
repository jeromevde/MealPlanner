# Salmon Sweet Potato Plate
<!-- quick:20 -->

![Salmon Sweet Potato Plate](images/thursday_evening_1.jpg)

Roast {200g {sweet_potato}} until tender. Pan-sear {150g {salmon}} in {10g {olive_oil}}. Serve with {100g {broccoli}} (steamed) and a squeeze of {5g {lemon}}. Finish with {1g {black_pepper}}.

# Salmon Sweet Potato Plate
<!-- quick:20 -->

![Salmon Sweet Potato Plate](images/thursday_evening_1.jpg)

Roast {200g {sweet_potato}} until soft and browned. Stir {8g {mustard}}, {5g {maple_syrup}}, {5g {lemon}} juice, and {5g {olive_oil}} into a glaze. Pan-sear {150g {salmon}} in {5g {olive_oil}}, then brush with the glaze in the last minute so it lacqueres instead of burning. Serve with {100g {broccoli}} tossed in the leftover pan glaze and finish with {5g {dill}} and {1g {black_pepper}}.

# Shrimp Bulgur Bowl
<!-- quick:15 -->

![Shrimp Bulgur Bowl](images/friday_evening_2.jpg)

Cook {130g {bulgur}}. Soften {80g {bell_pepper}} and {4g {garlic}} in {10g {olive_oil}}, then stir in {8g {harissa}} and {2g {cumin}} to bloom the spices. Add {140g {shrimp}} and cook just until pink and coated. Fold through the warm bulgur with {8g {lemon}} juice so the harissa becomes a light sauce on the grains. Crumble in {25g {feta_cheese}} while still warm, then finish with {5g {mint}} and {5g {parsley}}.

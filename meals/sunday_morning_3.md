# Greek Yogurt Parfait
<!-- quick:5 -->

![Greek Yogurt Parfait](images/sunday_morning_3.jpg)

Stir {180g {yogurt}} with {10g {honey}}, {1g {cinnamon}}, and {8g {chia_seed}} until the seeds are fully coated. In a glass, layer with {70g {raspberry}} and {50g {blueberry}}, then {30g {oat}} and {20g {almond}} (chopped) mixed with a pinch of {2g {cocoa_powder}}. The cocoa should taste like a faint chocolate note, not a powdery top layer.

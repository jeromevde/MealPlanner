# Egg Drop Bone Broth
<!-- quick:8 -->

![Egg Drop Bone Broth](images/sunday_morning_1.jpg)

Bring {350g {chicken_broth}} with {5g {ginger}} to a boil. Slowly drizzle in beaten {80g {egg}} while stirring. Add {80g {spinach}} and {5g {sesame_oil}}. Serve with {120g {yogurt}}, {40g {sauerkraut}}, and {150g {kombucha}} on the side.

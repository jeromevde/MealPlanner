# Egg Drop Bone Broth
<!-- quick:10 -->

![Egg Drop Bone Broth](images/sunday_morning_1.jpg)

Simmer {350g {chicken_broth}} with {5g {ginger}}, {40g {shiitake_mushroom}} (sliced thin), and {8g {soy_sauce}} until the mushrooms are soft and the broth smells rich. Off heat, dissolve {10g {miso}}. Return to a gentle simmer and slowly drizzle in beaten {80g {egg}} while stirring so it forms silky ribbons. Wilt in {80g {spinach}}, then finish with {5g {sesame_oil}}, {3g {chili_oil}}, and {8g {scallion}}.

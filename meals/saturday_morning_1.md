# Beef Tartare Arugula
<!-- quick:12 -->

![Beef Tartare Arugula](images/saturday_morning_1.jpg)

Make a dressing by whisking {15g {egg_yolk}} with {5g {mustard}}, {5g {worcestershire_sauce}}, {10g {olive_oil}}, and {8g {lemon}} juice until creamy. Fold in {140g {beef}} (finely diced), {12g {shallot}} (minced), {5g {caper}} (chopped), {5g {scallion}}, {1g {salt}}, and {1g {black_pepper}} — the yolk should coat every piece. Pile over {100g {arugula}} and finish with shaved {25g {parmesan_cheese}} and toasted {12g {pine_nut}}.

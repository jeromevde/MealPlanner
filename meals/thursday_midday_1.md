# Miso Tofu Soup Bowl
<!-- quick:12 -->

![Miso Tofu Soup Bowl](images/thursday_midday_1.jpg)

Simmer {300g {chicken_broth}} with {5g {ginger}} and {50g {shiitake_mushroom}} until fragrant. Soften {80g {bok_choy}} in the broth, then add {150g {tofu}} (cubed) and {5g {wakame}} just to warm through. Take off heat and whisk in {30g {miso}} (never boil after this). Stir in {5g {sesame_oil}} and {2g {chili_oil}} for a glossy finish. Serve over {100g {brown_rice}} in the same bowl so the rice soaks up the broth, and scatter {10g {scallion}}.

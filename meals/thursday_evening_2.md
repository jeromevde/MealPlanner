# Cod Quinoa Bowl
<!-- quick:18 -->

![Cod Quinoa Bowl](images/thursday_evening_2.jpg)

Cook {120g {quinoa}}. Soften {100g {zucchini}}, {80g {tomato}}, {20g {black_olive}}, and {3g {garlic}} in {10g {olive_oil}} until the tomato collapses into a light sauce. Season with {2g {paprika}}. Nestle {140g {cod}} into the pan, cover, and cook gently until it flakes into the vegetables. Spoon over the quinoa with {8g {lemon}} juice and {5g {parsley}}.

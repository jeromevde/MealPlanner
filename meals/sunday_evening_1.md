# Smoked Salmon Beet Rye
<!-- quick:10 -->

![Smoked Salmon Beet Rye](images/sunday_evening_1.jpg)

Toast {80g {rye_bread}}. Beat {60g {goat_cheese}} with {5g {horseradish}}, {5g {dill}}, and {5g {lemon}} juice until spreadable and bright — this softens both the goat cheese and the salmon. Spread thickly, then layer thin {80g {beet}}, {80g {smoked_salmon}}, and {40g {cucumber}} ribbons. Add a handful of {50g {arugula}}, {5g {caper}}, toasted {8g {pine_nut}}, and {1g {black_pepper}}.

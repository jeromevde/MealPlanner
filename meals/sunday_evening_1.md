# Smoked Salmon Beet Rye
<!-- quick:10 -->

![Smoked Salmon Beet Rye](images/sunday_evening_1.jpg)

Toast {80g {rye_bread}}. Spread with {60g {goat_cheese}}. Layer {80g {beet}} (thinly sliced), {80g {smoked_salmon}}, and {50g {arugula}}. Finish with {8g {pine_nut}}, {5g {lemon}} juice, and {1g {black_pepper}}.

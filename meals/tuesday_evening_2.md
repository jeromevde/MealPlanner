# Salmon Lentil Bowl
<!-- quick:18 -->

![Salmon Lentil Bowl](images/tuesday_evening_2.jpg)

Warm {130g {lentil}} with {3g {garlic}}. Whisk {40g {yogurt}} with {5g {mustard}}, {5g {lemon}} juice, {8g {olive_oil}}, and {5g {dill}} into a creamy dressing. Fold most of it into the warm lentils with {80g {spinach}} so the greens wilt and the lentils turn saucy. Pan-sear {140g {salmon}} in {8g {olive_oil}} until the skin is crisp. Serve over the lentils with the remaining dressing and {1g {black_pepper}}.
